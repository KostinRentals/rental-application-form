3 Simple Steps to Apply

  1. **Download & Print**

    Click the **Download Application Form (PDF)** button above and print all **3 pages** of the form. [cite_start]Please remember to print all legal names clearly[cite: 3].

  2. **Fill Out Completely**

    Complete every section of the form in ink. This includes:

    • Full Applicant and Non-Applicant Details.
    • Employment and Previous Residence information.
      • [cite_start]
    • Initialing your choice for the **Credit Check Requirement**[cite: 38, 39, 40, 43].
      • [cite_start]
    • **Signing and Dating** the final page[cite: 45].
    **Important Pet Policy:** No cats are allowed. [cite_start]Only dogs will be considered with prior written approval (please detail on a separate sheet)[cite: 35].
  3. **Submit via Email**

    Scan or take a clear photograph of all **3 completed and signed pages** of the application. Attach the files and send them to the submission email address below.

Submit Your Application to kostin.rentals@gmail.com

We will contact you shortly after reviewing your submission.
